Areas of Expertise & Experience

With a deep-seated belief in the power of innovation to improve lives, I am dedicated to developing intelligent technologies as a budding Embedded Software Developer and aspiring AI Engineer.

Libraries & Frameworks

Skilled in developing data-driven solutions using NumPy, Pandas, scikit-learn, and OpenCV, with a focus on intuitive front-end design via React. I am currently advancing my capabilities in deep learning with TensorFlow and backend engineering within the .NET ecosystem.

Embedded Systems Engineering

My work centers on the seamless integration of hardware and software within embedded environments. Utilizing C++ to program ESP32, Arduino, and Raspberry Pi, I engineer autonomous robotics and custom automation solutions from the initial PCB design to final system deployment.

Web & App Development

I specialize in building end-to-end web applications, combining a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript with a focus on React for creating high-performance user interfaces. On the backend, I leverage C#, Java, and Python to build secure, efficient systems, and I am currently advancing my capabilities in the ASP.NET ecosystem to deliver enterprise-grade web solutions.

Automation

I engineer intelligent automation by leveraging n8n to orchestrate complex, event-driven workflows. I specialize in integrating disparate APIs and web services into unified ecosystems, prioritizing operational efficiency and data integrity across all platforms.

Hardware Engineering

Implementing end-to-end electronic systems, I translate conceptual ideas into high-end PCB designs using KiCad. My process prioritizes in-depth electronic analysis and schematic integrity, transitioning seamlessly from rapid prototyping to production-ready hardware implementation.

Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

I build end-to-end AI solutions, bridging the gap between raw data and intelligent action. By integrating the power of NumPy, Pandas, and scikit-learn for robust analysis with OpenCV for computer vision, I build systems that perceive and predict. I am currently scaling these capabilities through TensorFlow, focusing on the development of deep learning models that drive autonomous and automated systems.

Graphic Design

I bridge the gap between technical functionality and visual aesthetics. Leveraging the Adobe Creative Suite(Lightroom & Photoshop), Canva, and PicsArt, I deliver professional-grade digital assets and agile branding. I am currently deepening my expertise in Figma to design modern, user-centric interfaces (UX) that elevate my web and application development projects.

Community Engagement & Leadership

Beyond engineering, I am dedicated to community impact and leadership. As a representative of the MOIC Club at Kırıkkale University, I advocate for social responsibility through organized community service and teamwork-driven projects. I focus on bridging diverse perspectives to build a more engaged and collaborative campus environment.
